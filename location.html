The Isadora - North Goa

The Isadora by Greenambit is located at Calangute, North Goa. The road to project is beautifully shadowed with palm trees giving truly a feel of housing in Goa. Isadora, despite being part of main city is located at serene corner of Calangute that keeps you very much near to market, restaurants, clubs, beach, ATM, petrol pump etc. These 1BHKs are near Calangute Beach at a distance of 8 minutes drive.

Don't miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to buy your very own apartment in North Goa. This luxuriant apartment nestled amidst the natural beauty of Goa will be something that will earn you some brownie points amongst your friends and family! And of course, it will prove to be a worthy real estate investment. We bet, this astounding prospect of owning a splendid 1BHK in Calangute, Goa is already making you smile with joy!
