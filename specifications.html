High End Specifications

The development of apartments in Goa is designed with greenery in neighborhood right from the entry of project. Special care has been given to the designing of the project. Units open to beautiful green views right from the balcony or private terraces. It makes your home a much more meaningful place bringing peace and serenity right where you wake up & sleep. The twin towers of 1 BHK apartments in The Isadora are officially named as One Banyan & Two Banyan. The construction is progressing at a good pace at site and the view from units can be enjoyed from same.
